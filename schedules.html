Festival Schedule

Plan your visit to the Science and Technology Festival with our comprehensive schedule of events and activities. The festival runs on June 28th, 2025.

June 28, 2025

Time Event Location Description
9:00 AM Registration Opens Main Entrance Check-in and collect your festival passes
10:00 AM - 11:30 AM Opening Ceremony Main Auditorium Official opening with guest speakers
11:45 AM - 1:00 PM Hands-on Science Activities Exhibition Hall Various interactive science demonstrations
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Lunch Break Cafeteria Food and refreshments available for purchase
2:00 PM - 3:30 PM Paper Rocket Workshop Main Hall Create and launch your own paper rockets
3:45 PM - 4:45 PM Slime Making Workshop Room B12 Fun with slime creation and chemistry
5:00 PM - 6:30 PM Closing Ceremony & Awards Main Auditorium Presenting awards and closing the festival

Important Notes

  • All activities are included with your festival admission.
  • Some workshops have limited capacity and are first-come, first-served.
  • The schedule is subject to minor changes. Check information boards at the venue.
  • Please arrive 15 minutes early for workshops and presentations.
  • Information desks are located at the Main Entrance and Exhibition Hall.
