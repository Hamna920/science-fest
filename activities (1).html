Festival Activities

Discover a wide range of hands-on activities designed to inspire and educate. From exciting experiments to interactive demonstrations, there's something for everyone!

Featured Activities

creativity and innovation

Get involved in making paper rockets to showcase science in a fun and engaging way !

Location: Main Hall

Ages: All ages

Slime making

Engage in slime making with our fun and exciting slime making event for all ages.

Location: Room B12

Ages: 10+

Microscopy Workshop

Discover the microscopic world and learn how to use scientific equipment. Examine cells, microorganisms, and fascinating microscopic structures.

Location: Science Lab 3

Ages: 12+

Robotics Workshop

Build and program simple robots in this introductory robotics workshop. Learn the basics of coding and engineering principles.

Location: Technology Suite

Ages: 14+

Chemistry Demonstrations

Watch our chemistry experts perform spectacular demonstrations showcasing chemical reactions and principles.

Location: Chemistry Lab

Ages: All ages

Virtual Reality Experience

Experience science and technology through cutting-edge virtual reality applications. Travel through space, dive into the ocean, or explore the human body.

Location: Digital Hub

Ages: 8+
